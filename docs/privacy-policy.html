Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

CloudClip ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Clipboard Content: When you enable automatic clipboard sync, the extension captures text that you copy. Before uploading, we automatically redact sensitive information including:

  • Social Security Numbers (SSN)
  • Credit card numbers
  • Passwords and API keys

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password.

Device Information: We collect a randomly generated device identifier and optional device name to help you identify which device each clipboard item came from.

How We Use Your Information

  • To synchronize your clipboard across your devices
  • To display your clipboard history in the extension popup
  • To identify which device captured each clipboard item
  • To provide customer support when requested

Data Storage & Security

Your clipboard data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud platform. We implement the following security measures:

  • Row-Level Security (RLS): You can only access your own data
  • HTTPS Encryption: All data is transmitted over encrypted connections
  • Client-Side Redaction: Sensitive data is removed before upload
  • Secure Authentication: Short-lived session tokens, no embedded secrets

Important: We never store your clipboard data in plain text if it contains detected sensitive information. SSNs, credit cards, and passwords are automatically replaced with [REDACTED] before upload.

Your Rights & Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Disable Auto-Capture: Turn off automatic clipboard sync at any time in settings
  • Delete Items: Remove individual clipboard items with a long-press
  • Delete All Data: Permanently erase all your clipboard history from our servers
  • Account Deletion: Contact us to completely remove your account

Data Retention

We retain your clipboard data until you choose to delete it. When you delete items, they are:

  • Immediately removed from the visible interface
  • Soft-deleted from the database (hidden but recoverable for 30 days)
  • Permanently deleted after 30 days

If you uninstall the extension, your device record is automatically removed, but your clipboard history remains accessible from other devices until manually deleted.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (Privacy Policy)

Permissions Explained

  • clipboardRead: Required to capture copied text for syncing
  • clipboardWrite: Enables one-click re-copy from history
  • storage: Stores preferences and cached data locally
  • Host access: Required to detect copy events on web pages

Children's Privacy

CloudClip is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
